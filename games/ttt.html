Ultimate Tic Tac Toe

Ultimate Tic Tac Toe is a two player board game where you battle it out on 9 Tic-Tac-Toe boards. Your opponent plays on the grid corresponding to your last played move. If you place a cross in the top left corner of a small grid then they play on the top left grid. Win three small grids in a line to Win!
